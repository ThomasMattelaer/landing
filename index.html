Learn to ride the city as you never did !

The Fixie Bike Bootcamp is an intensive program designed to teach basic to advanced fixie riding techniques through hands-on lessons and group rides with experienced instructors.

Discover more.

How it works :

Hands-On Instruction

Participants will receive hands-on instruction and guidance from experienced fixie instructors. This will include lessons on basic riding techniques, as well as advanced tricks and stunts.

Group Rides

Participants will have the opportunity to practice and perfect their skills through group rides with other bootcamp attendees. This will provide a supportive and collaborative learning environment.

Skill Development

The bootcamp is designed to help participants develop their fixie riding skills, whether they are a beginner or a seasoned rider. The combination of hands-on instruction and group rides will provide participants with the opportunity to build their confidence and mastery of the bike.
